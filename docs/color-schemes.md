# Color Schemes

## Warm Stone / Amber-Neutral

This was the warmer palette pass that leaned more ivory, parchment, and stone.

### Core Tokens

- `--bg: #f7f2e8`
- `--bg-top: #f3ede1`
- `--bg-bottom: #ebe4d5`
- `--fg: #1f1f1f`
- `--gray-light: #f8f4ed`
- `--gray-dark: #78716c`
- `--accent: #1c1917`
- `--accent-soft: #292524`
- `--highlight: rgba(253, 230, 138, 0.35)`

### Section Treatment

- Hero: warm cream gradient with amber glow
- Demo: soft white / cream gradient
- Layers: deeper cream / tan gradient
- Footer: dark stone

### Overall Feel

- Ivory / parchment / stone
- Warmer and more buttery
- Less green, more amber

## Forest / Sage / Cream

This is the older scheme we returned to, based on the earlier committed landing direction.

### Core Tokens

- `--bg: #f7f2ea`
- `--bg-top: #f8f4ec`
- `--bg-bottom: #f5efe6`
- `--fg: #10261d`
- `--gray-light: #f7f2ea`
- `--gray-mid: rgba(24, 53, 42, 0.12)`
- `--gray-dark: rgba(16, 38, 29, 0.64)`
- `--accent: #10261d`
- `--accent-soft: #18352a`
- `--highlight: rgba(88, 121, 100, 0.16)`

### Section Treatment

- Hero: cream gradient with subtle sage tint
- Demo: white
- Layers: cream
- Footer: deep forest

### Overall Feel

- Forest / sage / cream
- Cleaner and slightly greener
- Closer to the older Vanna landing identity
